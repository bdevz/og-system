## Metrics definitions

- **Response Rate:** Percentage of submissions that get any response within 7 days
- **Ghost Rate:** Percentage of submissions with no follow-up after 14 days
- **Submissions:** Total submissions through this vendor over tracking period
- **Placements:** Successful placements resulting in consultant engagement
- **Known End Clients:** Types of companies this vendor typically recruits for

## Thresholds for action

- **Ghost Rate > 50%:** Vendor likely inactive or unreliable. Escalate for human review.
- **Response Rate < 40%:** Vendor not responsive. Consider lower priority.
- **Tier 1:** +1.5 confidence boost in scoring
- **Tier 2:** +0.0 confidence (neutral)
- **Tier 3:** -1.0 confidence penalty
- **Unknown:** Escalate to EM for manual research

## Update schedule

- Update weekly based on submission outcomes
